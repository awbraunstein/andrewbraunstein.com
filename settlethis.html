Let's Settle This!

Settle petty arguments with your friends quickly and easily. Combining the infinite knowledge of Wolfram Alpha, easy mobile to mobile connections via Bump, and the seamless mobile payment system Venmo, Let's Settle This allows you to put your money where your mouth is.
